Sports and Sports Facilities


Sports:-


There are many sports avilable in Montfort School:-


1)-Badminton
Badminton Coaching : Badminton is practiced on Tuesday, Thursday & Saturday
Time : 4:00 to 6:00 p.m

2)-Cricket
Cricket Coaching: Cricket is practiced on Tuesday, Thursaday and Saturday from 04:00 p.m. to 06:00 p.m..
Students from classes IV onwards are free to avail this facility.

3)-Football
Football Coaching : For Montfortians
Days : Monday to Friday
Time : 6:00 to 7:00 a.m.
For Other School Children
Days : Monday to Saturday
Time : 5:00 to 6:30 p.m.

4)-Basket ball
Basket Ball Coaching: Children are free to join Basket ball coaching classes as mentioned below.
Girls:
Summer : 6:00 to 7:30 a.m.
Winter : 6:30 to 8:00 a.m.
Boys:
Summer : 4:00 to 7:00 p.m.
Winter : 4.00 to 7:00 p.m.

5)-Skating
Students have to enroll themselves to join the skating coaching classes by paying an annual membership fee.
Days : Monday - Friday
Time : 5:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m. (Summer) and 4:00 to 5:00 p.m. (Winter)

6)-Swimming
Swimming: Students have to enroll themselves paying by an annual membership fee. Swimming Pool remains open from 1st April to 30th September.
Girls : Tuesday, Thursday and Saturday
Boys : Monday, Wednesday and Friday
Time : 4:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m. (Duration 45 minutes)

7)-Lawn Tennis
Lawn Tennis
Days : Monday to Saturday
Time : 4:00 p.m. to 6:00 p.m.
