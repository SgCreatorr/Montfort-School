Montfort School

Montfort School is one of the best schools in Delhi. Montfort school has swimming pool, basketball court and many more sports facilities. The founder Saint Louis Marie de Montfort were a French priest and a Catholic saint. In year 2020 we celebrated 50th anniversary of our school. At Montfort, they at "Total Education" which implies a holistic approach to education and which is concerned with the total development of an individual as a person. We prepare these young minds to become a nation builder by inculcating moral and cultural values.
